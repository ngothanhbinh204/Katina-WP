Private Equity

Katina Capital Partners combines deep transaction expertise with active ownership to transform promising businesses into market leaders. We don’t just allocate capital—we engineer lasting value. Our Private Equity practice partners with management teams to drive growth, improve operational efficiency, and position companies for successful exits. From deal origination to portfolio enhancement, we apply a safety‑first philosophy, rigorous governance, and the discipline of institutional investors.
